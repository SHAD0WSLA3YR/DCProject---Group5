3D Heart

Pulmonary trunk: Artery that carries deoxygenated blood from the right ventricle to the lungs for oxygenation.

Right ventricle: Chamber of the heart that pumps deoxygenated blood to the pulmonary trunk for oxygenation in the lungs.

Descending aorta: The largest artery in the body that carries oxygen-rich blood from the heart's left ventricle to the rest of the body.

Right pulmonary artery: Branch of the pulmonary trunk that carries deoxygenated blood to the right lung for oxygenation.
